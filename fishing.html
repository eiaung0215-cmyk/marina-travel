RECREATIONAL FISHING

4,500,000 VND

4 hours/Train

Fishing reflects the nature of a man: the more difficult the challenge, the more persistent he becomes in conquering it, even if he spends the whole day fishing only to release the fish back into the lake at the end of the day, he still finds joy in it.

Fishing is not just a hobby, but also a passion. What could be better than sitting on a luxurious yacht, drifting on the open sea, watching the bustling scenery of the ocean and ships alongside close friends?

Marina Travel will provide you with fishing rods and bait; your only task is to catch as many fish as possible and bring home as much joy as you can.

Tourists will travel on canoes heading out to sea, accompanied by experienced and professional fishing captains who will assist them with fishing spots and techniques. Four hours at sea will provide tourists with a truly new and unique experience, satisfying their passion for fishing.
